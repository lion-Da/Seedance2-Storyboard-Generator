0-3s: Scene establishment - Wide shot of mountain path at sunset (@图片3), golden orange light, long shadows, forest silhouettes against colorful sky, Wu Song (@图片2) walking unsteadily up the path, camera high angle showing isolated figure, wind blowing leaves

3-6s: Discovery - Medium shot as Wu Song approaches mountain shrine (@图片4), weathered wooden structure with official proclamation (@图片6) posted, camera pushes in toward the text, Wu Song's face leaning in to read, expression shifting from drunk confusion to shock

6-9s: Realization and conflict - Close-up of Wu Song's face (@图片2), sweat appearing on forehead, eyes widening with fear, internal conflict visible, camera quick cuts between his face and the tiger warning text, dramatic side lighting emphasizing his dilemma

12-15s: Rest - Wide shot of large flat rock (@图片7) under moonlight, Wu Song lying down, staff dropped beside him, fast asleep, forest surrounding with mysterious shadows, stars appearing in dark sky, serene but dangerous atmosphere, text overlay "醉卧青石"
