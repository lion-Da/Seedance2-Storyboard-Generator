## Seedance Prompt

3D animated style similar to Kung Fu Panda, vibrant colors, dramatic lighting, 15 seconds, 9:16 aspect ratio, heroic and energetic atmosphere

0-3s: Scene establishment - Wide shot of Jingyang Ridge inn exterior, red lanterns fluttering in wind, "三碗不过冈" signboard clearly visible, sunny day with blue sky, camera slowly pushes in towards the entrance, welcoming and lively atmosphere

3-6s: Character introduction - Wu Song (@图片1) strides into the inn, tall and muscular, wearing green battle robe, confident heroic stance, camera tracks his movement from behind, then cuts to medium shot as he sits at table and places wooden staff with a loud thud, powerful presence

6-9s: Action sequence - Montage of Wu Song drinking, innkeeper (@图片2) pouring wine from jar (@图片1), Wu Song lifting bowl (@图片2) and drinking in one gulp, slamming bowl down, repeatedly, camera quick cuts between pouring, drinking, emptying bowls, building excitement and rhythm, warm lantern light of interior (@图片4)

9-12s: Climax moment - Close-up of Wu Song's flushed face, fierce heroic expression, 18 empty bowls visible on table, innkeeper counting with shocked expression, Wu Song stands up swaying slightly but still powerful, camera dramatic upward angle showing his dominance

12-15s: Conclusion - Wu Song grabs his staff and walks toward door, back view, innkeeper watching with concern, warm interior light transitioning to bright exterior, sense of impending adventure, text overlay "三碗不过冈"
